# gofal.wales — Business Context

This document gives AI full context on the business model, not just the product.

---

## Business Model

**What:** Wales' first Welsh-language care home directory and marketplace.

**Revenue streams:**
| Tier | Price | Value |
|------|-------|-------|
| Free listing | £0/mo | Basic CIW data, shown in search |
| Enhanced listing | £29/mo | Description, photos, fee info, priority placement |
| Featured listing | £49/mo | Top of search, badge, highlighted card |

**Strategic play:** Every claimed listing is a warm lead for **Ateb AI voice agents** at £300-500/month. The directory is the Trojan horse — it gets care homes in the door. The voice agent pitch closes the deal.

**Unit economics (targets):**
- CAC (Cost to Acquire Claimed Listing): £0 (organic SEO + outreach emails)
- Free → Enhanced conversion target: 10-15%
- Enhanced → Ateb upsell target: 20-30%
- LTV of Ateb customer: £3,600-6,000/year
- Monthly churn target: <5%

## Ideal Customer Profiles

### ICP 1: Family (consumer)
- **Who:** Welsh-speaking adults aged 40-60
- **Situation:** Searching for care for an elderly parent
- **Pain:** Can't find care homes that offer Welsh-language care
- **Trigger:** Parent's health decline, hospital discharge, dementia diagnosis
- **What they need:** Compare homes, check CIW ratings, find Welsh speakers, enquire
- **How they find us:** Google search (Welsh + English), word of mouth, social care referrals

### ICP 2: Care Home Manager (provider)
- **Who:** Registered managers or operators of care homes in Wales
- **Pain:** Hard to differentiate, low online visibility, compliance pressure on Active Offer
- **Trigger:** Seeing their listing on gofal.wales, CIW inspection mentioning Welsh language
- **What they need:** More enquiries, showcase Welsh provision, look professional online
- **How they find us:** Outreach emails, seeing their listing, industry networks

### ICP 3: Ateb Upsell Target
- **Who:** Claimed care home operators handling 50+ calls/month
- **Pain:** Missed calls, no Welsh-speaking receptionist, after-hours enquiries
- **What they need:** AI voice agent that answers in Welsh + English 24/7
- **Trigger:** Seeing enquiry data from gofal.wales demonstrating demand

## Sales Pipeline

```
Stage 1: Listed (automatic — all ~1,000 CIW homes)
    ↓ Outreach email sequence (3 emails over 14 days)
Stage 2: Aware (opened email / visited listing)
    ↓ Claim CTA on profile page
Stage 3: Claimed (verified email ownership)
    ↓ Profile completion prompts (automated 7-day reminder)
Stage 4: Active (profile completed, responding to enquiries)
    ↓ Enhanced listing upsell (based on enquiry volume)
Stage 5: Paying (£29-49/month subscriber)
    ↓ Ateb demo invitation (based on call volume / enquiry data)
Stage 6: Ateb Customer (£300-500/month voice agent)
```
